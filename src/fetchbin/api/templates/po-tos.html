Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

Information We Collect

When you use FetchBin, we collect the following information:

  • Content you choose to share (command outputs)
  • IP addresses for voting and rate limiting purposes
  • Timestamps of when content is shared
  • Commands associated with your outputs (if provided)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide the FetchBin service
  • Prevent abuse and spam through rate limiting
  • Enable the voting system (one vote per IP)
  • Display shared content to other users

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your shared content is public by default unless you mark it as hidden. IP addresses are never displayed publicly and are only used for technical purposes.

Data Retention

Shared content is stored indefinitely unless you delete it using the delete token. You can delete your shares at any time by using the delete URL provided when sharing.

Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at GitHub Issues.
